Gustavus is pretty small and has relatively few ammenities. It can be hard to get around without a car, it's uncertain whether/how many restaurants will be open, and there are a limited number of ways to even get there. With that in mind, we have come up with a kind of template itinerary for most folks, have a local caterer hired to feed people for the weekend, and have reserved the Gustavus Community Center (GCC). IMPORTANT NOTE: GCC doesn't like outdoor shoes in the building, so please bring a pair of comfortable indoor shoes (unless you're comfortable spending a lot of time on concrete in socks).

Here's how we envision the weekend!

Friday, June 16, 2023
Arrival Day

Attire
Whatever you're wearing

Most guests arrive in Gustavus on Alaska Airlines 157, around 5 PM. They'll get to their lodging and from there go to the GCC (transportation TBD) for dinner. Pick up a sack breakfast for tomorrow.

Saturday, June 17th, 2023
Tour (or hangout) day!

Attire
Layers and binoculars

This is your chance to see Glacier Bay! We recommend taking the Glacier Bay Lodge's tour boat. It's an all day sort of deal. They give you lunch. We'll figure out transportation to/from Bartlett Cove. No rush to book. Prepare for glaciers and puffins.

Wedding prep and sandwiches at GCC for those who aren't on the boat. Dinner at GCC for everyone.
